üéØ Course Description

BNAD 303 provides students with an exciting opportunity to learn about the basics of Marketing and its function in business. This course covers marketing programs, strategies, and theories essential for business success.

üåü Course Learning Outcomes

Upon completion, you will be able to:

  • Recognize and analyze ethical issues in business
  • Understand strategic implications in global business
  • Describe consumer decision-making processes
  • Identify marketing mix components
  • Understand market segmentation
  • Explain importance of digital marketing and social media
